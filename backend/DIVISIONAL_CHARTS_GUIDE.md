# Divisional Charts (Shodashvarga) - Complete Guide

This document explains all 16 divisional charts that are now included in the Kundali PDF with detailed descriptions.

## ✅ What's Been Added

Each divisional chart now includes:
- **Full Name & Purpose**: Clear title showing what the chart represents
- **Detailed Description**: 3-4 sentences explaining:
  - What the chart reveals about your life
  - Specific areas it governs
  - How it influences your destiny
  - Practical applications

## 📊 All 16 Divisional Charts Explained

### 1. **D1 - Rasi Chart (Lagna Chart)**
**Focus**: Overall Life & Personality
- Main birth chart - foundation of all analysis
- Shows physical body, general life path, major events
- Most important chart for all predictions

### 2. **D2 - Hora Chart**
**Focus**: Wealth & Prosperity
- Financial fortune and money accumulation
- Inheritance patterns and material resources
- Timing of financial gains/losses

### 3. **D3 - Drekkana Chart**
**Focus**: Siblings & Courage
- Relationships with brothers, sisters, cousins
- Communication skills and mental strength
- Ability to overcome obstacles

### 4. **D4 - Chaturthamsa Chart**
**Focus**: Property & Assets
- Real estate, land, vehicles, ancestral wealth
- Home ownership and property inheritance
- Relationship with mother

### 5. **D7 - Saptamsa Chart**
**Focus**: Children & Progeny
- Fertility, number of children, their well-being
- Timing of childbirth and conception challenges
- Creative abilities

### 6. **D9 - Navamsha Chart**
**Focus**: Marriage & Dharma
- Second most important chart
- Marriage quality, spouse nature, marital happiness
- Spiritual path and life purpose
- Essential for compatibility analysis

### 7. **D10 - Dashamamsa Chart**
**Focus**: Career & Profession
- Professional success, reputation, authority
- Suitable career paths and promotions
- Public image and recognition

### 8. **D12 - Dwadamsha Chart**
**Focus**: Parents & Ancestry
- Relationship with parents (especially father)
- Ancestral blessings and karmic inheritance
- Family traditions and spiritual heritage

### 9. **D16 - Shodashamsha Chart**
**Focus**: Vehicles & Comforts
- Automobiles, luxury items, physical pleasures
- Quality and number of vehicles
- Material possessions and worldly comforts

### 10. **D20 - Vimshamsha Chart**
**Focus**: Spiritual Progress
- Spiritual development and religious practices
- Meditation abilities and mantra power
- Connection with divine energies

### 11. **D24 - Chaturvimshamsha Chart**
**Focus**: Education & Learning
- Academic achievements and intellectual capacity
- Success in studies and higher education
- Scholarships, honors, teaching abilities

### 12. **D27 - Saptavimshamsha Chart**
**Focus**: Strengths & Weaknesses
- Inherent physical and mental strengths
- Areas needing improvement
- Resilience and ability to overcome adversities

### 13. **D30 - Trimshamsha Chart**
**Focus**: Misfortunes & Evils
- Potential dangers, accidents, legal troubles
- Hidden enemies and negative influences
- Karmic debts and preventive measures

### 14. **D40 - Khavedamsha Chart**
**Focus**: Maternal Heritage
- Maternal lineage and mother's family influence
- Blessings from maternal side
- Inheritance from mother

### 15. **D45 - Akshavedamsha Chart**
**Focus**: General Well-being
- Overall character, conduct, moral values
- Reputation and how others perceive you
- General auspiciousness and lucky periods

### 16. **D60 - Shashtiamsha Chart**
**Focus**: Past Life Karma
- Most subtle and important for karma analysis
- Soul's journey and spiritual debts
- True strength of planets
- Root cause of life events
